Engineering and Maintenance Management

Maintenance Engineering is the discipline and profession of applying engineering concepts for the optimization of equipment, procedures, and departmental budgets to achieve better maintainability, reliability, and availability of equipment. Maintenance engineers are certified to install, troubleshoot, and repair specific types of equipment. .
