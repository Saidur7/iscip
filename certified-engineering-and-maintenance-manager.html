Certified Engineering and Maintenance Manager

Course Description: Engineering is about designing processes and making products to solve real-world problems. Our course enables you to develop your engineering knowledge, skills, imagination and experience to the highest levels in readiness for your future career. The main objective of this program is to develop and enhance the participants on the platform of Maintenance Management. This certification programme has been customized based on the industrial requirements to make the participants role ready for Maintenance functions. The participants are trained by industry experienced faculties on Business Management Systems and Standards with Auditing process, Application of Core Tools, Problem Solving Techniques, TPM, Basic Leadership skills and with shop floor training in industries.

What you will learn: The following specialisations are available within our certification course of engineering and management department:
Aerospace and Aerothermal Engineering
Bioengineering
Civil, Structural and Environmental Engineering
Electrical and Electronic Engineering
Electrical and Information Sciences
Energy, Sustainability and the Environment
Information and Computer Engineering
Instrumentation and Control
Mechanical Engineering.
