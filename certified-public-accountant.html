Certified Public Accountant - CPATM

Course Description: Certified Public Accountant (CPA) is a globally recognized designation and is the highest standard of competence in Accountancy. Equip yourself with essential skills and learn from the professional standards in the accounting industry.

What you will learn: It covers accounting topics and other topics like law, analytics, taxation, auditing, evaluation, regulatory frameworks and procedures, ethics, planning, and management of finances.
Course Instructor: Professor Dr. Asif Salam
Program structure: 3 Months - 4 hours online class per week using zoom platform | Total Sessions 48
Course Fees: CAD$ 2000

Who will get benefits: All Business and finance graduates, engineers, commerce and account students, economist and operation people will get knowledge to perform their duties with excellence.
